Biography

The Story

I’m Alex, known on the decks as LXBE (but the fam still calls me ALEXBE). Born and raised in Barcelona, I grew up tuned into the hum of my city — every street performer, vinyl shop, and underground party fed an obsession with rhythm that never switched off.

Music isn’t a hobby; it’s how I move through life. From childhood I’ve heard stories in every sound around me, and today I translate that connection into DJ sets that invite people to feel deeply. Afrohouse and house anchor my selections, while funky house and nu disco slip in to keep the energy magnetic, soulful, and full of warmth.

What matters most is the crowd — watching them let go for a few hours, breathe with every drop, and walk away with a memory tied to the music. That’s my mission every night: craft unforgettable experiences where emotions and rhythm move together.
